# The Ultimate Guide to Our Signature Sandwiches

What makes a **great sandwich**? Is it the bread? The filling? The sauce? At TuckInn Proper, we believe it's all three—plus something intangible: passion.

We started TuckInn because we were tired of sad, soggy sandwiches that left us hungry an hour later. We knew lunch could be better. It *should* be better. Here's how we craft our **signature sandwiches** to be the best in the city.

## Our Sandwich Philosophy

### 1. Bread is Everything
Great sandwiches start with great bread. We:
- Source from local bakeries whenever possible
- Toast bread to add texture and prevent sogginess
- Match bread type to filling (hearty breads for hearty fillings)
- Bake our own specialty rolls for signature items

### 2. Quality Ingredients Matter
We never compromise on quality. Our ingredients are:
- **Farm-fresh vegetables** delivered daily
- **Premium proteins** sourced responsibly
- **Artisan cheeses** selected for flavor profiles
- **House-made sauces** with no preservatives

### 3. Balance is Key
Every sandwich needs:
- **Protein** to keep you full
- **Vegetables** for crunch and nutrition
- **Sauce** for moisture and flavor
- **Acidity** to brighten everything (pickles, vinegar, citrus)
