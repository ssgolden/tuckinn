# Office Catering 101: How to Order Lunch for Your Team

Organizing **office catering** can feel overwhelming. You've got 25 team members with different preferences, dietary restrictions, and lunch schedules. The pressure is on to make everyone happy while staying within budget.

Relax—we've got you covered. As **corporate catering** experts, we're sharing everything we've learned about feeding teams of all sizes.

## Why Office Catering Matters

Before we dive into the logistics, let's talk about why team lunches are worth the investment:

- **Team building** - Shared meals create bonds
- **Increased productivity** - Well-fed teams perform better
- **Employee satisfaction** - Quality perks retain talent
- **Meeting efficiency** - Lunch & learn sessions
- **Company culture** - Rituals that define workplaces

## Step-by-Step Catering Guide

### Step 1: Head Count and Preferences
**When:** 3-5 days before the event  
**Who:** Team lead or office manager

Get accurate numbers:
- ✅ Total attendees
- ✅ Remote vs. in-office people
- ✅ Dietary restrictions/allergies
- ✅ Preferred cuisine types
- ✅ Budget per person

**Pro tip:** Create a simple form with Google Forms or similar. Send to team for easy data collection.

### Step 4: Handle Dietary Restrictions

**The golden rule:** Always offer options for common dietary needs.

| Dietary Need | Percentage | Solution |
|--------------|------------|----------|
| Vegetarian | ~25% | 3-4 options |
| Vegan | ~5% | 1-2 options |
| Gluten-free | ~10% | Labeled options |
| Dairy-free | ~8% | Clear labeling |
| Nut allergies | ~2% | Nut-free prep area |
| Kosher/Halal | ~3% | Special arrangements |

**How to collect this info:**
```
Survey question: "Do you have any dietary restrictions 
we should know about? (Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, 
allegies, etc.)"
```

---

### Step 5: Timing is Everything

**Best delivery times:**
- **Working lunch:** Deliver 10 minutes before meeting starts
- **Team lunch:** Deliver when people arrive (prevents food sitting)
- **All-day event:** Stagger deliveries (lunch at noon, snacks at 3 PM)

**Setup time needed:**
- Buffet style: 15-20 minutes
- Individual boxes: 5-10 minutes
- Drop-off (no setup): No additional time

---

### Step 6: Logistics Checklist

❓ Do you have enough tables/serving space?  
❓ Are plates/napkins/utensils included?  
❓ Do you need serving utensils?  
❓ Where will you put trash/recycling?  
❓ Who's responsible for cleanup?  
❓ Do you need ice/beverages? (Often overlooked!)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Ordering Too Late
**The problem:** Rushed orders = limited options
**The fix:** Order 48 hours ahead minimum

### ❌ Ignoring Portions
**The problem:** Running out of food or massive waste
**The fix:** Order 10% more than headcount

### ❌ Forgetting the Details
**The problem:** No plates, napkins, or drinks
**The fix:** Create a complete checklist

### ❌ Not Having a Backup Plan
**The problem:** Late delivery, missing items
**The fix:** Get caterer's direct contact number

### ❌ Skipping Labels
**The problem:** "Is this the vegetarian one?"
**The fix:** Request clearly labeled items
