1. Go to: https://business.google.com
2. Sign in with Google account
3. Click "Add your business to Google"

### Step 2: Enter Business Info

| Business Name | **TuckInn Proper** |

| Website | **https://tuckinnproper.com** |

### Step 3: Verification Options

**Option A: Postcard (Most Common)**
- Google sends postcard to address (3-5 days)
- Contains 5-digit verification code
- Enter code online to verify

**Option B: Phone Verification**
- Available for some businesses
- Instant verification
- Call to +34 627 755 609 with code

**Option C: Email Verification**
- Available for some domains
- Instant if eligible

## Step 5: Enable Features

### Turn On Messaging
1. GBP Dashboard → "Customers" → "Messages"
2. Turn toggle ON
3. Set up auto-reply:
   ```
   "Thanks for messaging TuckInn Proper! We typically respond 
   within 10 minutes. View our menu: https://tuckinnproper.com"
   ```
4. Messages will come to your WhatsApp

### Set Up Q&A
1. GBP Dashboard → "Customers" → "Questions"
2. Add common questions:
   - Q: "Do you offer gluten-free options?"
     A: "Yes! Many items can be made gluten-free."
   - Q: "What's your most popular sandwich?"
     A: "Our Classic Club is a customer favorite!"
   - Q: "Do you cater for events?"
     A: "Yes! We offer office catering. Email or call us."

## Step 8: Reviews (Start Getting Them!)

### Get Your First Reviews
1. Tap "Reviews" in GBP app
2. Share your review link:
   ```
   https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=YOUR_PLACE_ID
   ```
3. Send to:
   - Friends and family (ask for honest reviews)
   - First customers (ask politely after order)
   - Local business connections

### Review Reply Strategy
- ✅ Reply to ALL reviews (positive and negative)
- ✅ Thank positive reviewers by name
- ✅ Address negative reviews professionally
- ✅ Offer to fix issues

**Example Positive Reply**:
```
"Thanks Maria! We're so glad you enjoyed the Mediterranean 
Wrap. We look forward to serving you again soon! 🙏"
```

**Example Negative Response**:
```
"Hi John, we're sorry your order wasn't perfect. We'd love 
to make it right. Please call us at +34 627 755 609 or 
message us directly. Thanks for the feedback!"
```
